About Us

D. Butlion and Associates is a distinguished legal practice founded by Dorota Barbara Butlion, LLB, admitted as an Attorney in 1990, bringing over 35 years of invaluable experience to the legal landscape.

Since establishing her own practice in 1998 and maintaining a consistent presence in Milnerton for the past 21 years, Ms. Butlion has built a reputation for her thoroughness, dedication, and strategic approach to legal matters.

Our mission is to provide comprehensive and compassionate legal solutions, particularly in the complex areas of family law and general civil litigation. We are committed to guiding our clients through challenging times with clarity, integrity, and a deep understanding of the law. We pride ourselves on our ability to navigate the intricacies of the Magistrates', Regional, and High Courts, ensuring our clients' rights are robustly defended.
